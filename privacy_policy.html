PuckDuel: Privacy Policy

PuckDuel is developed by Shubham Pawar. It is a local Wi-Fi multiplayer air hockey game. Your privacy is respected and protected.

Data Collected

PuckDuel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All gameplay (puck physics, paddle positions, scores) is communicated directly between two devices on the same local Wi-Fi network using UDP. No data ever leaves your local network.

Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
android.permission.INTERNET Required for local network (Wi-Fi) communication between two devices on the same network.
android.permission.CAMERA Used optionally to scan a QR code displayed on the host device to connect quickly. No images are stored or transmitted.

How Your Data Is Used

No personal data is collected or used. The camera is accessed only when you choose to scan a QR code to join a game, and no images are saved. Network communication stays entirely within your local Wi-Fi.

Third-Party Services

PuckDuel does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud services.

Children's Privacy

PuckDuel does not collect any data from users of any age.
